# YourNextAway

YourNextAway is a football travel planning app built around the fixture first.

Instead of starting with flights or hotels, the app starts with the match. Users can browse fixtures across major European leagues and UEFA competitions, explore clubs, stadiums and cities, then plan a full trip around the game they want to attend.

## Core product direction

YourNextAway is designed for neutral football travellers planning short breaks around matches, not away-end travel.

The app brings together:

- fixture discovery across domestic leagues and UEFA competitions
- club, stadium and city context
- match-level ticket difficulty and trip planning guidance
- trip building and trip workspace flows
- wallet-style trip organisation
- affiliate travel and experience links
- fixture follow and alert logic

## Current stack

- Expo
- React Native
- Expo Router
- TypeScript

## Product principles

- fixture-first planning
- realistic travel guidance
- clear estimated pricing language
- no fake affiliate fallbacks
- launch-quality data coverage across supported leagues
